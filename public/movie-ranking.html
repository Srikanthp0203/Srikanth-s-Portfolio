The Best Movies According to Srikanth

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Martian

This is my favorite Sci-fi movie. I love that its fun and exciting, a thrilling spectacle with deep emotional payoffs and strong performances from its all-star cast..

Jurassic World

It was great seeing old and new characters and how they come together. easy-to-follow story line. kept my attention throughout. I became invested in the characters.

Gravity

Simplistic, fast phase, emotional roller coaster that delivers pure deep space terror; nerve wracking suspense; outstanding cinematography, and awe inspiring performance by Sandra Bullock and George Clooney.
